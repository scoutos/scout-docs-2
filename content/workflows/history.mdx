# History

History gives you an audit trail of workflow revisions, authors and deployment status.

## What You Get

- Revision timeline with timestamps
- Author visibility for each change
- Revision naming and descriptions
- Restore capability for prior versions

## Revision Workflow

1. Make changes and test in Console
2. Name important revisions clearly
3. Add short descriptions for intent
4. Promote revision by environment

## Naming Guidelines

Good revision names explain the intent, not just the block changed.

- Good: `Add fallback path for CRM timeout`
- Avoid: `updated blocks`

## Restore and Rollback

If a deployment causes issues:

1. Open History
2. Find last known good revision
3. Restore and redeploy

This gives you fast rollback without rebuilding from scratch.
