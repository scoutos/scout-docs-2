# Quick Start

Get a personal AI assistant running in about five minutes. Your assistant will help you set itself up—just ask.

**Prerequisites:** A Scout account (free plan available)

---

## Step 1: Get Started with a Template

The fastest way to create your agent is with a pre-built template.

1. Open **Marketplace** in Scout Studio
2. Find the **Personal Agent** template
3. Click **Use Template**
4. Complete the **12-question interview**

This interview defines your agent's personality, capabilities, and preferences. It helps your assistant understand how it can be most productive for you.

---

## Step 2: Connect Your Calendar

Your assistant can connect tools for you—no need to navigate settings menus.

**Ask your assistant:** "Help me connect my calendar"

The assistant walks you through the integration. Once connected, it can check your schedule, find meeting times, and remind you of upcoming events.

---

## Step 3: Connect Your Email

Let your agent read, summarize, and draft emails.

**Ask your assistant:** "Help me connect my email"

The assistant guides you through connecting Gmail, Outlook, or another provider.

---

## Step 4: Connect Your Chat Platform

Talk to your agent where you already work—Slack or Telegram.

**Ask your assistant:** "Help me connect Slack" or "Help me connect Telegram"

Your assistant will help set up the chat platform integration. This is where your agent will communicate with you day-to-day.

---

## Step 5: Schedule Your Daily Brief

Set up a routine that keeps you informed every morning.

**Ask your assistant:** "Set up a daily brief every morning at 8 AM that:"

- Checks my email
- Reviews my calendar
- Searches for news on topics I care about
- Posts the summary to my [Slack/Telegram] channel

Your assistant creates the schedule for you—no need to configure it manually.

---

## Key Principle

The assistant is equipped to help you integrate with all core tools. You shouldn't need to dig through settings—just ask.

---

## Tips

- **Start simple** — Add tools one at a time so you can test each connection
- **Be specific** — Clearer instructions = better results
- **Chat regularly** — The more you use it, the better it gets
- **Ask for help** — Your assistant can configure itself, schedule tasks, and connect tools
