# Quick Start

Build your personal AI assistant in about five minutes. Connect your calendar, email, and team chat so your agent can help you stay on top of your day.

## What You'll Build

A personal agent that can:
- Check your calendar and find meeting times
- Read, summarize, and draft emails
- Send you alerts and respond to messages on Slack or Telegram

**Prerequisites:** A Scout account (free plan available)

---

## Step 1: Create Your Personal Agent

Head to the [Agent Marketplace](https://studio.scoutos.com/agents/marketplace) to browse templates, or build from scratch.

### Using a Template

1. Find the **Personal Assistant** template
2. Click **Install**
3. Give your agent a name like "My Daily Assistant"
4. Add a description: "Helps me manage my calendar, email, and stay organized"

### Building from Scratch

1. Go to [studio.scoutos.com/agents/new](https://studio.scoutos.com/agents/new)
2. Name your agent and add a description
3. Add instructions like:

```
You are my personal assistant. Help me stay on top of my day by:
- Checking my calendar when asked
- Summarizing important emails
- Drafting responses when I need them
- Reminding me of upcoming meetings

Be concise and proactive. If you see something important, flag it.
```

4. Save your agent

---

## Step 2: Connect Your Calendar

Connect Google Calendar or Outlook so your agent can check your schedule and find meeting times.

1. Go to **Integrations** in your agent settings
2. Click **Connect** next to Google Calendar or Outlook
3. Authorize the connection
4. Return to your agent and enable the calendar tool

### What This Enables

- Check your schedule for any day
- Find available meeting times
- Prepare for upcoming meetings with context

### Quick Test

Ask your agent:

> "What's on my calendar today?"

Or try:

> "Do I have any conflicts next Tuesday afternoon?"

---

## Step 3: Connect Your Email

Connect Gmail or Outlook email so your agent can read, summarize, and draft messages.

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Connect** Gmail or Outlook
2. Authorize the connection
3. Enable the email tool in your agent settings

### What This Enables

- Summarize unread emails
- Draft responses for your review
- Find specific emails by topic or sender
- Track action items from threads

### Quick Test

Ask your agent:

> "Summarize my unread emails from today"

Or try:

> "Draft a reply to the email from Sarah about the project deadline"

---

## Step 4: Connect Slack or Telegram

Choose your preferred platform to chat with your agent throughout the day.

### Connect Slack

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Connect** Slack
2. Select your workspace and authorize
3. Choose which channels your agent can access
4. Enable the Slack tool in your agent settings

### Connect Telegram

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Connect** Telegram
2. Start a chat with the Scout bot
3. Follow the authorization flow
4. Enable the Telegram tool

### What This Enables

- Send you proactive alerts and reminders
- Respond when you mention it
- Be your daily companion for quick questions
- Deliver scheduled briefings

### Quick Test

Send your agent a message:

> "Remind me in 30 minutes to take a break"

Or try:

> "What should I prepare for my next meeting?"

## Next Steps

Now that your personal agent is set up:

### Schedule Daily Briefings

Have your agent send you a morning summary automatically. Go to **Settings** → **Schedule** and set up a daily run.

Example briefing instructions:
```
Every morning at 8am, send me:
- Today's calendar events
- Urgent emails from yesterday
- Any action items I should prioritize
```

See [Agent Scheduling](/agents/scheduling) for full details.

### Customize Instructions

Refine your agent's behavior by editing its instructions. Make it more proactive, change its tone, or focus on specific types of tasks.

### Explore More Integrations

Connect additional tools:
- **Notion** — Search your notes and docs
- **GitHub** — Track issues and pull requests
- **Salesforce or HubSpot** — Monitor your pipeline

Browse all available integrations in the Scout dashboard.

## Tips for Success

- **Start with a few tools** — Add more as you learn what your agent handles well
- **Be specific in instructions** — The clearer you are, the better your agent performs
- **Test often** — Chat with your agent regularly to refine its behavior
- **Use scheduling** — Let your agent work autonomously with scheduled tasks
