# About Scout

Scout is an AI agent platform from ScoutOS for teams that want to automate complex work across their existing tools.

The product is designed for practical, high-leverage workflows such as meeting prep, CRM hygiene, competitive research, support enablement, and recurring operations tasks.

## What You Can Build with Scout

- **Agents** that reason through multi-step tasks and use connected tools.
- **Workflows** that trigger, route, and orchestrate repeatable processes.
- **Integrations** across common sales, productivity, and engineering systems.
- **Data context** through Drive and structured sources so agents can answer with relevant, current information.

## Who Uses Scout

Scout is used by startups and enterprise teams across GTM, engineering, and operations.

Examples and case studies are published on the [Scout customers page](https://www.scoutos.com/customers), including teams such as Statsig, Dagster, Quipli, and Wide Awake.

## How Scout Supports Teams

Scout emphasizes white-glove onboarding and implementation support:

- defining workflows and success criteria,
- connecting integrations and data sources,
- launching into production usage,
- and refining behavior over time as requirements evolve.
